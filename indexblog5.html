3D Printing Knight Chesspiece using Ultimaker 2P

Designing with Autodesk Fusion 360

Using the concept of Vector Graphics, I drew the outline of the knight with the guide of an actual picture. Then, I extruded and fillet edges of the 3D figure. To add the base, I added a rectangle vertically on the bottom and revolved it. Thus, creating the Knight chesspiece design.

3D Printing with Ultimaker 2P

I uploaded the 3D design as a 3MF file onto a sd card. Proceed to upload the design onto Cura to slice it. The SD card is then, placed into the Ultimaker 2P to be printed and the result is the Knight chesspiece.
